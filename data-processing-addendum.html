Data Processing Addendum (DPA)

This DPA describes the processing of personal data when Fluxa acts as a processor on behalf of a controller.

Roles

Controller: the customer. Processor: Fluxa.

Subprocessors

We may engage subprocessors (hosting, analytics, payment) under written contract and require data protection obligations.

Security

We implement technical and organizational measures appropriate to the risk.

Data subject rights

We will assist controllers in responding to requests from data subjects.
